The Biography Of My Favorite Celebrity Asa Asika

My famous most talented Nigeria talent manager as i fondly call him and co-founder of the company, The plug Entertainment. He has been active since 2012 and has continued to set the pace in the music business. He hails from Anambra state. Asa was born on August 20, 1990 in Lagos State.He attended White Sands school, Lekki, graduating in 2008. After his secondary education, he decided not to further his education at a higher institution because he did not see higher education as a pathway to success. He is famous for been Davido's manager.
